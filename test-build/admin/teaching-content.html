Teaching Philosophy Statement

Pedagogical Approaches Documentation

Document and organize your teaching methodologies and TESOL approaches

Task-Based Language Teaching (TBLT)

Learner-centered approach focusing on meaningful communication through authentic tasks and real-world problem solving.

Key Implementation Strategies:
  • Pre-task preparation and planning
  • Task cycle with report and analysis
  • Language focus on form
  • Authentic outcome assessment

Project-Based Learning (PBL)

Extended learning experiences that engage students in complex, real-world projects requiring English language use across all four skills.

Current Projects:
  • Community research documentaries
  • Cross-cultural business proposals
  • Academic conference presentations
  • Digital storytelling portfolios

Communicative Language Teaching (CLT)

Emphasis on interaction as both the means and goal of learning, with focus on meaningful communication over accuracy.

Classroom Techniques:
  • Information gap activities
  • Role-play and simulations
  • Jigsaw reading tasks
  • Collaborative problem solving

Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL)

Dual-focused educational approach integrating content learning with language development through subject matter instruction.

Subject Areas:
  • Business and Economics
  • Environmental Science
  • International Relations
  • Digital Media Studies

Technology-Enhanced Language Learning (TELL)

Strategic integration of digital tools and platforms to enhance language learning through multimedia, interactivity, and authentic communication.

Technology Integration:
  • Virtual reality cultural immersion
  • AI-powered pronunciation feedback
  • Collaborative online writing platforms
  • Digital portfolio assessment

Culturally Responsive Teaching

Pedagogical approach that recognizes, respects, and uses students' cultural backgrounds as assets in the learning process.

Core Practices:
  • Cultural asset mapping
  • Multilingual resource development
  • Cross-cultural communication training
  • Community partnership building
